WhimFolk is an Ottawa-based guitar duo bringing a rich blend of genres to the local stage. Founded in 2015, the band features Jason Fleischer (vocals, guitar) and Mark Boycott (guitar, percussion). Known for their versatile repertoire, WhimFolk weaves together folk, blues, country, and rock from timeless classics to modern favorites. Whether performing at a bustling farmers' market or a local venue, their arrangements create the perfect atmosphere for any crowd.
